Some Interesting Information About Me


What kind of photographer am I? I always say I'm a nature photographer, but when it comes down to it... I love it all!

Since I was a boy I've always been attracted by photography's world, any kind, and by how a simple photo managed to stir up in me emotions or impress memories in my mind.

I am a interesting mix of analytic process and creative fire that fuels my drive to learn more and grow constantly.

Sometimes torn between the roles of the fabricator and observer, I bring to any endeavor the perfect mix of the creativity and practicality.

I thrive in creative and collaborative environments where hard work and passion for taking great pictures is valued.
